Beer bottle

Attributes:
bottle_is_open (boolean)
alcohol_by_volume (float)
beer_style (string)
international_bitterness_units (integer)
price (float)
fluid_volume_in_oz (float)

Methods:
discount_price (lowers price attribute)
empty (lowers fluid_volume_in_oz attribute)
fill (increases fluid_volume_in_oz attribute)
open_bottle (changes bottle_is_open boolean from false to true)
