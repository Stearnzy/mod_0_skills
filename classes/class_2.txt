Smoothie

Attributes:
name (string)
ingredients (array)
has_lid (boolean)
total_capacity (float)
current_capacity (float)
temp_at_creation (integer)
current_temp (integer)

Methods:
add_ingredient (adds ingredient to ingredients array)
toggle_lid (adds or removes lid)
rename (changes 'name' string)
calculate_amount_drank (shows difference in volume between total_capacity and current_capacity)
calculate_change_in_temp (uses current_temp and temp_at_creation to find change in temperature)
